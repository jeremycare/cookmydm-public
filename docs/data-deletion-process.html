User Data Deletion Instructions

In accordance with Facebook and Instagram platform requirements, this page explains how users can request the deletion of their personal data collected by our service.

What Data Can Be Deleted

You may request the deletion of any data we have collected, including:

  • Messages you have sent to our Instagram page
  • Videos you have submitted
  • Any associated metadata stored in our database

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, simply follow one of the steps below:

  • Send us a direct message on Instagram with the text: "Delete my data"

What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your deletion request, we will:

  • Identify all data associated with your Instagram account
  • Permanently delete it from our database
  • Confirm to you via Instagram DM once the deletion is complete

Contact

If you have questions or need assistance with data deletion, please send us a direct message through our Instagram page.
