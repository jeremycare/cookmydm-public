Privacy Policy - CookMyDM

We value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

When you send messages or videos to our Instagram page, we collect:

  • The messages and videos you send us.
  • Information extracted from your content to process and provide recipes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Analyzing your videos to extract cooking recipes.
  • Allowing you to request and retrieve recipes previously submitted.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except when necessary for technical processing (e.g., cloud services or AI tools used to analyze videos).

4. Data Storage

Your messages and videos are securely stored in our database. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through Instagram DM.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request the deletion of your data.
  • Contact us with any questions regarding how your data is used.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please send us a direct message on our Instagram page.
